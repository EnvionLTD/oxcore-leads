Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 May 2026

Who we are

Oxcore ("we", "us", "our") connects homeowners in Greater Manchester with local pressure washing professionals. This policy explains how we handle your personal data when you use our website or submit a quote request form.

What we collect

  • Name — so the professional knows who to contact
  • Phone number — so they can arrange your quote
  • Postcode — to match you with someone in your area
  • Service required — so they know what you need

How we use it

  • To pass your details to a vetted local pressure washing professional who will contact you to arrange a free, no-obligation quote
  • To improve our service and understand which areas have demand

Who we share it with

We share your details only with a local pressure washing professional in your area for the purpose of arranging your quote. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Advertising and tracking

We use Meta (Facebook) Pixel and Conversions API to measure the performance of our ads and improve our service. This may involve sharing hashed (anonymised) data with Meta to match ad interactions. You can opt out of personalised ads in your Facebook ad preferences.

How long we keep it

We keep your data for up to 12 months after your enquiry. After that, it is deleted.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To make a request, email us at the address below.
